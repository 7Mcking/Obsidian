# Electrical Subsystem
Electrical subsystem is responsible of taking care of power and computational requirements of the system. It manages the power available and distribution.

## System Level Requirements on Electrical Subsystem
1. Detect separation of the satellite from the launch vehicle
2. Receive and store power for future use
3. Provide adequate and regulated power to all components onboard on the satellite
4. Provide necessary computation power
5. Monitor status of the satellite
6. Provide data storage
7. Respond to telecommands from Uplink
