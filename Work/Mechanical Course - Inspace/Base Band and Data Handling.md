## Ground Resolution and Data Rate
Time take to move a d distance (IT) = $d/V_{g}$
Total number of pixel = $N$
Total number of bits to be shifted = $N * \frac{number of bits}{pixel}$
Data rate = $N *\frac{No of bits per pixel}{IT}$
