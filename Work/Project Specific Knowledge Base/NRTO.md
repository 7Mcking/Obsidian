# Drone requirements
## Platform and Propulsion:
Design: Hexacopter or Quadcopter
Propulsion: Hybrid (ICE + Battery)
Endurance (Flight Time): 2 hours
Altitude Capability:
Max Take-off Altitude: 3 km ASL
Max Flight Altitude: 1 km AGL
Cruising Speed: 10 m/s
Operating Temperature: -10 deg C to 45 deg C


## Operations and Environment:
Take-off/Landing Area: VTOL from small cleared areas of 30 m x 30 m
Wind Resistance: Withstand sustained winds of 15 knots during all flight operations

## Communications and Navigation:
Datalink Range: 25 km
Datalink Security: Must have inbuilt redundancy/backup and be preferably encrypted
Navigation Resilience: Must have the capability for GPS-denied navigation (e.g., using
INS or Vision-Based systems)
Safety Features: Integrated Return to Home (RTH) and Fail-Safe Mode functionality

# EOIR Requirements

Frequency Range: LWIR
Weight: <3kg
Power Consumption: <50W
Thermal Core: 640 × 512 Uncooled sensor
Pixel Pitch: <20 µm
Thermal Lens Options: >18 mm
Zoom Capability: 10× optical
Resolution @ 1 km (GSD): 30–50 cm
